# Product

## Register

product

## Users

~40 party guests, one night only, on their phones. One-handed use, dim ambient
lighting, alcohol, noise, short attention. They are "secret agents" being dealt
real-life missions. The host uses a separate admin page, also likely on a phone.
Nobody reads instructions; every screen must be self-evident at a glance.

## Product Purpose

Handler One is a one-night "Casino Royale" party mission game. Players register
an alias, get dealt missions, report Complete / Failed / Fold, and scores stay
hidden until the host flips a synchronized dramatic reveal. Success = the game
runs itself all night with zero support questions, and the reveal lands as a
theatrical moment.

## Brand Personality

Discreet, engraved, high-stakes. "Le Cercle Privé": a private Monte Carlo salon
in 1932 — engraved bearer certificates, brass plates, one lamp over green baize.
Luxury through linework and restraint, never through more gold. Spy-flavoured
copy is rationed, not sprinkled.

## Anti-references

- Vegas slot-machine kitsch: neon, glitter, chip-and-dice clipart, looping shine.
- Generic AI casino defaults: flat felt-green gradient + Georgia serif + gold everywhere.
- Party-invite energy: bouncy animation, confetti, emoji.

## Design Principles

1. **Printed, not stamped-on** — opulence lives in hairlines, symmetry, and
   engraving discipline; gold is seasoning (≤5% of any screen).
2. **Two color layers that never compete** — gold marks *where* a boundary is
   (structure); green/red mark *what will happen* (signal).
3. **One deliberate gesture per event** — motion is precise and final; nothing
   loops, nothing bounces. The dealt card is the app's signature motion.
4. **Dim-room legible, thumb-first** — verified contrast on every pairing,
   ≥56px tap targets, minimal reading per screen.
5. **Rationed homage** — the theme lives in the graphic language; button copy
   stays plain except the few loaded lines the spec itself calls for.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum; body pairings verified in DESIGN.md (most hit AAA).
- Ivory focus ring on every interactive element (never gold — gold is deletable).
- Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives; content never gated on animation.
- Meaning never carried by color alone (icons/copy accompany red warnings).
